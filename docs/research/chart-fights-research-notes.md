# Chart-Fights Research Notes: Similar Products and Analogs
Date: 2026-06-26
Task: Research PvP/competitive stock trading games, simulators with multiplayer, MOBA/finance crossovers, time accel mechanics, voice chat + trading, historical replay games. Focus on 1v1, sabotage/abilities, accelerated time, chat. Note historical vs live data. Creative analogs. Names, links, desc, overlaps, lessons.

## Summary
Few perfect matches for "chart-fights" (1v1 PvP stock trading with sabotage abilities, accel time, chat on charts/historical or live). Closest are head-to-head backtesting/PvP replay platforms (ChartChamps, FXR Battles, TraderFi tournaments) and gamified duel apps (Hedgd). 

Strong overlaps in:
- Historical replay for fair PvP (same data, pure skill).
- 1v1 or small-group battles/duels/tournaments.
- Leaderboards and competition.
- Some time compression or manual control (scrub/speed).
- Text/voice chat in competitive modes.
- Simulated or live markets.

Gaps (opportunities for uniqueness):
- Direct sabotage or "abilities" (cooldowns, powers affecting opponent/chart) are rare or absent in finance sims.
- Resource-managed fast-forward bars uncommon (more free speed sliders or progression unlocks).
- Full MOBA-like (lanes=assets?, team abilities, ganks/sabotage) non-existent.
- Voice chat rare but powerful when present (FXR).
- Most are educational/leaderboard-focused or solo backtesting; few true direct 1v1 "fights" with interaction.

Many use **historical replay** for training/competition (fair, repeatable, no real money risk). Others use **live/real-time prices** or fully simulated economies.

Creative analogs from non-finance: deckbuilders for abilities/sabotage (Insider Trading), RTS for economy/time management, poker for info asymmetry/bluffing, fantasy sports for portfolio leagues.

## Top Similar Products

### 1. ChartChamps (PvP Trade Backtesting)
- **Link**: https://chartchamps.com/ (acquired by Tradeify ~Jun 2026)
- **Description**: Competitive platform for traders to compete head-to-head in live, Elo-ranked 1v1 matches and tournaments using historical market data on TradingView charts. Randomly selected sessions (bull/bear/sideways). Match replays, global leaderboards, daily challenges, bracket/group tournaments. All simulated, no real money.
- **Overlaps**: Direct 1v1 PvP on charts, historical replay for fair comparison, tournaments, replays for review. Backtesting turned into sport/competition.
- **Data Type**: Historical replay (random past data segments unfold or replayed).
- **Lessons/Ideas**: 
  - Steal: Same historical segment for both players = pure skill test (avoids market variance). Built-in replays for post-fight analysis and learning.
  - Avoid or differentiate: Limited direct interaction (pure performance comparison); add sabotage/abilities to make "fights" more dynamic.
  - Great for accelerated sessions via replay controls.
- Citations: Web results on acquisition and mechanics [web:45], [web:46].

### 2. TraderFi (trader2b.com / TraderFi game)
- **Link**: https://trader2b.com/stock-market-game/ ; play at play.traderfi.game
- **Description**: Free trading game/sim using real historical market data replays. Time-compressed: full trading day in minutes. Start with $100k virtual. Solo practice or compete in real-time challenges, invite friends, create/host tournaments, climb leaderboards (profit/points/win rate). Supports stocks, FX, commodities, crypto. Skill-based, tokens/rewards.
- **Overlaps**: Historical replay + multiplayer/competitive elements (challenges, tournaments). Accelerated time (compressed playback). Chat/invite features implied for friends/tournaments. Fast-paced sessions.
- **Data Type**: Historical replay (actual past sessions).
- **Lessons/Ideas**:
  - Steal: Time-compressed replays for quick, engaging "fights" without waiting real time. Tournament creation/hosting for private 1v1 or groups. Multi-asset support.
  - Potential for sabotage: Tournaments could add rules or shared events.
  - Strong for historical authenticity vs live volatility.
- Citations: Detailed site content [web:69], [web:33].

### 3. FXR Battles (FX Replay)
- **Link**: https://fxreplay.com/battles ; app.fxreplay.com
- **Description**: Head-to-head trading competitions on real historical FX (and other?) price data that unfolds in real-time during battles. Modes: Time Trial (precision under clock), Profit Target (first to target). 1v1 duels or group. Custom battles (balance, assets, duration, drawdowns). Live voice chat (strategy or trash talk) + text chat. Elo leaderboard. "Mental warfare." For Discord groups, prop firms, events. No real money.
- **Overlaps**: Historical replay + real-time unfolding in PvP. Direct competition. **Voice chat + text chat** during battles. Time-based or target-based "fights". Custom rules.
- **Data Type**: Historical replay (real past data, played back live-speed in competition).
- **Lessons/Ideas**:
  - Steal: Live voice chat massively boosts engagement, psychology, and "fight" feel (trash talk, coordination). Real-time unfolding vs static replay.
  - Custom setups for variety (1v1 stock-specific or multi-asset).
  - Ideas to steal/avoid: Mental/psych element; add visual "sabotage" via shared events or abilities.
- Citations: Site details [web:72], [web:84], [web:85].

### 4. Hedgd: Stock Market Game (iOS/Android app)
- **Link**: https://apps.apple.com/us/app/hedgd-stock-market-game/id6760241733 ; Google Play equivalent.
- **Description**: Gamified interactive trading game. Sandbox: $10k virtual, live US stocks/crypto prices. Arcade: Chart Scrubber (high-speed historical candlestick prediction, streaks, multipliers – "one wrong call and over"). Duels: 24-hour 1v1 battles on specific stock. Weekly tournaments with virtual prizes. Learning path (stages, XP, ranks), quizzes, portfolio charts, haptic scrubbing. Fantasy sports-style for stocks.
- **Overlaps**: 1v1 duels (time-limited), tournaments. Chart time control (scrubbing = manual fast-forward/rewind/explore). Competitive on specific assets. Live prices in main mode, historical in training.
- **Data Type**: Live prices (sandbox/duels); historical candlesticks (arcade scrubber).
- **Lessons/Ideas**:
  - Steal: Asset-specific 1v1 duels (focus "chart fights"). Chart scrubbing for interactive time control. Streak/gamification for engagement. Short duel duration (24h or less for snappier).
  - Time management via manual scrub – inspire resource bar for accel.
  - Combine learning with PvP.
- Citations: App Store description and updates [web:71], [web:50].

- **Overlaps**: Direct global PvP/competition. Accelerated time (season/economy compression). News as info. Potential sneaky/collective play via firms. Portfolio management.

- **Lessons/Ideas**:
  - Steal: Seasonal resets for fresh starts. Firm mechanics for team play or subtle sabotage/collusion. Dividend accel for long-term feel in short sessions. "Be sneaky" as hook.

## Creative Analogs (Non-Perfect but Inspirational)

- **RTS games** (StarCraft, Age of Empires): Economy/resource management, time speed controls (often free or researched), abilities/units as "sabotage" (harass opponent), replays. Fast-forward in some modes.
- **MOBA** (LoL): 1v1 lanes possible in customs, abilities with cooldowns, ganks/sabotage, team chat/voice, info asymmetry (fog of war). Adapt: "Lanes" as stock sectors, abilities as chart tools or events.
- **Fantasy Sports** (ESPN, DraftKings leagues/head-to-head): Portfolio-like team building, 1v1 or league PvP on real performance data, chat, accelerated seasons. Historical or live events.
- **Among Us / Social deduction**: Sabotage mechanics, chat/voice, info asymmetry, deception in "trading" context.
- **Deckbuilders/Roguelikes** (Slay the Spire, Balatro, Insider Trading): Perks/cards as abilities, synergies for powerful effects (market manipulation), runs for progression.
- **Fighting games**: Pure 1v1, timing/execution, abilities/movesets, mental warfare, replays.
- **Prediction markets** (Polymarket, Kalshi): PvP-ish on events, real data-ish.
- **Tycoon games** (Railroad Tycoon, Capitalism): Stock mechanics, player interactions, economy sims.

## Mechanics to Steal or Avoid; Specific Ideas
**Steal**:
- Historical replay segments for balanced 1v1 (ChartChamps, FXR, TraderFi, Trade Bots).
- Time compression or controllable speed (TraderFi compressed days; Trade Bots upgrades for days/sec; Hedgd scrub).
- Voice + text chat in matches (FXR – huge for "fights" energy).
- 1v1 specific focus (Hedgd duels on one stock) or quick sessions (Trading Game battles).
- Progression/unlocks for abilities (Trade Bots speed/automation; Insider Trading cards/perks).
- Custom games/tournaments/lobbies (MarketWatch, TraderFi).
- News/events or info asymmetry (Yale peeks, Tycoon news, Insider Trading manipulation).
- Firm/collective mechanics or "sneaky" options (Tycoon).
- Leaderboards + replays for retention and skill improvement.
- Resource for time (inspire: spend virtual "energy" or profits to fast-forward bars).

**Avoid or Differentiate**:
- Pure leaderboard grind without direct opponent interaction (feels less "PvP fight").
- Overly educational/classroom tone if targeting gamers.
- Simulated random markets if historical replay available (authenticity).
- No direct sabotage – this is the white space (add via abilities: rumor cards, forced shorts, chart disruptions, info denial).
- Long real-time waits; use accel or replays.

**Unique Ideas for Chart-Fights**:
- **Abilities/Sabotage**: Buy/use "powers" (e.g., leak rumor to move price, jam opponent's indicators, peek opponent's positions, short-squeeze event, cooldown-based chart hacks). Resource cost (cash or "sabotage points").
- **Time System**: Shared or individual "fast-forward bars" – spend resources to accelerate your view or the match clock. Pause mechanics with cost. Progression unlocks better bars.
- **MOBA Hybrid**: Multiple "lanes" (sectors/assets). 1v1 direct on main chart + side objectives (news events). Team abilities or ganks on shared market.
- **Info Asymmetry + Chat**: Private peeks or "scans" purchasable. Voice for bluffing/coordination while sabotaging.
- **Historical vs Live Modes**: Replay mode for pure skill; live for chaos; hybrid where sabotage affects replay playback.
- **Voice/Community**: Mandatory or opt-in voice in 1v1 for full psych element.
- **Analogs Implementation**: Deck of abilities like Insider Trading; RTS-style resource for time/abilities.

## Data Type Notes
- **Historical Replay**: TraderFi, ChartChamps, FXR Battles, Hedgd (arcade), Trade Bots, Yale (randomized). Benefits: Fair, educational, controllable speed, no external events bias.
- **Live/Real-Time Prices**: MarketWatch VSE, Hedgd (sandbox), many educational sims (SMG, HowTheMarketWorks), some apps. Benefits: Authentic feel, reacts to real news.
- **Simulated**: Stock Market Tycoon: Challenge (dynamic but generated).
- Most products emphasize no real money risk.
